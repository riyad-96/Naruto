Naruto Uzumaki

The story begins, when kaguya otsutsuki came to earth and met Lord Tenji.

After a long time, there is a village called Konoha. There were many beasts. one of the beast named Nine Tail fox, attacked the village. And a brave shinobi named Minato Namikaze saved the village by sacrificing his own life. He had a child named Naruto Uzumaki. He trained under the legendary sannin named Master Jiraya. He grown up as the finest shinobi in the world.
